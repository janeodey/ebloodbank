The eBloodBank project is a transformative initiative aimed at addressing the critical problem of blood unavailability for transfusions in Cross River State, Nigeria. This project seeks to revolutionize the way blood donations and transfusions are coordinated, providing a streamlined platform that connects registered hospitals with available blood donors in real-time.
